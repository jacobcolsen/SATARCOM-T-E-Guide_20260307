h2. What This Space Does

This Confluence space is the authoritative tracker for STARCOM T&E programs. It organizes 40 mandatory, conditional, and recommended tasks across four test phases:

* 🔵 *Pre-Test* — Planning, reviews, and certifications (~18 months to test start)
* 🔴 *During Test* — Execution, deficiency reporting, and status comms
* 🟢 *Post-Test* — Analysis, final reporting, and closeout
* 🟣 *Ongoing / Periodic* — Charter, TEMP, and DR management (continuous)
